Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer nationwide shipping across Pakistan. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee. All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your premium items arrive in perfect condition.

What is your return policy?

No Returns / All Sales Final. Due to the premium nature of our products, we do not accept returns or exchanges unless the item arrives damaged or defective. In such cases, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the issue.

How do I care for my Solo Wear garments?

To maintain the premium quality of your Solo Wear items, we recommend washing in cold water, using mild detergent, and air drying. Avoid bleach and iron directly on printed areas. Detailed care instructions are included with each purchase.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship exclusively within Pakistan. We are working to expand our international shipping options and hope to serve customers worldwide in the near future.
